# Screenshot Checklist (Store Submission)

## Required scenes (recommended)
1. Bilibili video page with floating button visible
2. Floating capture panel opened
3. Folder selection + custom tags input in capture panel
4. Popup settings (theme mode)
5. Manager page after saving videos (folder/video list)

## Capture rules
- Resolution:
  - Chrome/Edge: follow current dashboard minimum requirements
  - Firefox: at least one 1280x800 or larger screenshot
- Keep text clear, avoid blur/compression artifacts
- Avoid including personal/private account data in screenshots

## Suggested captions
- “One-click capture from Bilibili video pages”
- “Save to multiple folders with tags”
- “Fast local workflow with theme support”
